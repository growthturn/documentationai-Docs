## Troubleshooting Overview

Encounter issues with Carseto? This guide covers the most common problems and step-by-step fixes. Start with the basics like clearing your browser cache before diving into specific topics. Most issues resolve quickly without contacting support.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Always check your internet connection and try refreshing the page (`Ctrl+R` or `Cmd+R`) as a first step. Update your browser to the latest version for best compatibility.
</Callout>

## Account Login and Access Issues

Login failures often stem from cached data, forgotten passwords, or session timeouts. Follow these steps to regain access.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear Browser Cache" icon="trash-2">
    Clear cookies and cache specific to `carseto.com`.

    - In Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data
    - In Firefox: Options > Privacy > Cookies and Site Data > Manage Data > Search `carseto.com` > Remove All Shown

    Restart your browser after clearing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset Password" icon="key">
    Go to [https://carseto.com/login](https://carseto.com/login) and click "Forgot Password?".

    Enter your email. Check spam folder for the reset link (subject: "Reset Your Carseto Password").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Account Status" icon="shield">
    Ensure your account is not suspended. Log in via incognito mode to test.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="alert">
  If two-factor authentication fails, verify your authenticator app time syncs with your device clock.
</Callout>

## Search and Listing Display Problems

Searches for classics like "Porsche 911" or "Jaguar E-Type" not showing results? Display glitches affect filters or images.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop Browsers" icon="monitor">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Disable Extensions">
        Temporarily disable ad blockers or VPN extensions, as they block marketplace data.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check Console Errors">
        Right-click > Inspect > Console tab. Look for network errors.

````javascript
// Example error to watch for:
Failed to load resource: https://api.carseto.com/listings?query=porsche-911
````

        Report any `CORS` or `504` errors to support.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile Apps/Browsers" icon="smartphone">
    Force close the app or browser. Clear app cache via device settings.

    Ensure location services are enabled for regional listings (12 countries supported).
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Valuation Calculation Errors

Valuations for your garage cars updating incorrectly? Common with new listings or data sync issues.

<Expandable title="Advanced Valuation Troubleshooting" default-open="false">

Valuations pull from 5 European marketplaces, updated monthly. Errors occur if:

- Car details mismatch (e.g., "Mercedes-Benz 280 SL" vs "280SL")
- Rare models lack data

**Fix Steps:**

1. Verify car specs: Year, mileage, condition in your garage.
2. Refresh garage: [https://carseto.com/garage](https://carseto.com/garage) > Select car > Update Details.
3. Wait 24 hours for recalculation.

| Error Type | Cause | Solution |
|------------|-------|----------|
| "No Data" | Rare model | Add manual comps via "Document" tab |
| "Stale Value" | Sync delay | Trigger manual refresh |
| "Outdated" | Market change | Check `Last Updated` timestamp |

</Expandable>
