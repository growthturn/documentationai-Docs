## Recent Releases

Carseto releases new versions regularly to bring you the best experience in discovering, tracking, and managing classic cars. Check this page for detailed changelogs, including new features for your digital garage, marketplace enhancements, and performance improvements.

<Callout kind="info">
  Enable notifications in your account settings to get email alerts for major releases.
</Callout>

<Update label="2024-10-15" description="v1.2.0" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>
## New Features

- Added valuation trends graph in your digital garage, showing monthly market value updates for each car in your collection.
- Introduced provenance document upload with OCR scanning for service records and invoices.
- New sharing options let you generate public links to showcase individual cars or your entire portfolio.

## Improvements

- Search results now load `<2s` even with 50,000+ listings across Europe.
- Enhanced mobile support for adding cars from photos directly in the app.

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed valuation sync issues for non-EU currencies.
- Resolved garage export errors when including high-resolution images.
</Update>

<Update label="2024-09-20" description="v1.1.0" tags={["feature", "bugfix"]}>
## New Features

- Portfolio insights dashboard with total collection value and growth charts.
- Integrated 5 new European marketplaces, expanding listings to 45,000+ cars.
- Quick-add from popular searches like `Porsche 911` and `Jaguar E-Type`.

## Bug Fixes

- Corrected listing filters not persisting across sessions.
- Fixed authentication token refresh during long browsing sessions.
</Update>

<Update label="2024-08-10" description="v1.0.0" tags={["feature", "breaking"]}>
## New Features

- Launched core marketplace search across 12 countries.
- Digital garage for tracking personal classics with value estimates.
- Basic document storage for ownership history and photos.

## Breaking Changes

- Updated API endpoints from `/v0` to `/v1` for better scalability.
</Update>
