## Overview

Carseto provides a comprehensive platform for classic car enthusiasts. You can search over `45,000+` listings from major European marketplaces, build your digital garage, and connect with the community. Key features include advanced search tools, collection management, leaderboards, and daily updates to keep you informed on the best finds.

<Callout kind="info">
  All listings update daily from `5` European marketplaces across `12` countries.
</Callout>

## Browsing and Searching Listings

Discover classic cars effortlessly with Carseto's unified search across `45,887+` listings. Popular models like Porsche `911`, Mercedes-Benz `280 SL`, and Jaguar E-Type appear in trending searches.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Advanced Filters" icon="search" href="/docs/search-filters">
    Filter by make, model, price, location, and condition to find your perfect classic.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Popular Searches" icon="trending-up" href="/docs/popular-models">
    Jumpstart your search with curated lists of top models like Ferrari `308` and BMW M3.
  </Card>
</Columns>

Use these steps to perform a search:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Keywords" icon="search">
    Type a model like `Porsche 911` in the search bar at [carseto.com](https://carseto.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply Filters" icon="filter">
    Narrow results by country, price range (`<€50,000`), or mileage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Search" icon="bookmark">
    Bookmark results to receive alerts on new listings matching your criteria.
  </Step>
</Steps>
