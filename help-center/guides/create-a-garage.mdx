## Quickstart

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    ````
    Go to https://carseto.com and log in or create an account.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new garage">
    ````
    Click "New Garage" on your dashboard. Give it a name (e.g., "Classic Collection").
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first car">
    ````
    Click "+ Add Car" inside your garage. Fill in details and save.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
