## Overview

Adding a car to your Carseto garage is easy.

1. Go to your Garage dashboard.
2. Click the "+ Add Car" button.
3. Enter your car’s details (make, model, year).
4. Upload a photo (optional).
5. Click "Save" to finish.

Your car will appear in your garage instantly.
