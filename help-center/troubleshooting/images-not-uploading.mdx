---
title: Car images won't upload
description: Fix image upload problems in Carseto.
---

## Symptoms

- Image upload hangs or fails
- No image appears after saving

## Causes

- File is too large (max 5MB)
- Unsupported format (only JPG/PNG)
- Network issue

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check image size and format">
    ````
    Make sure the file is JPG or PNG, and less than 5MB.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Retry upload">
    ````
    Reload the page and try again.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
