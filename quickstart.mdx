## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">

Before starting, ensure you have:
- A valid email address for account verification
- Access to a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- Optional: Your classic car details (VIN, photos, service records) for adding to your garage

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up for a free Carseto account to access the marketplace and your digital garage.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Carseto" icon="globe">
    Go to [https://carseto.com](https://carseto.com) and click **Add Your Car** or **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a password, and complete the registration form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification email from Carseto and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the site, log in with your credentials, and access your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Search for Classic Cars

Explore over 45,000 listings from European marketplaces. Use the search bar for makes like Porsche or models like 911.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="By Make/Model" icon="search">
    Enter `Porsche 911` in the search bar to browse listings across 12 countries.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="By Category" icon="filter">
    Select categories like "Sports Cars" or "Coupes" from the Explore menu.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Popular Searches" icon="trending-up">
    Click popular links such as [Mercedes-Benz 280 SL](https://carseto.com/cars/mercedes-benz/280-sl) or [Jaguar E-Type](https://carseto.com/cars/jaguar/e-type).
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

For programmatic access, use the Search API:

<CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,cURL,Python">
  ```javascript
  const response = await fetch('https://api.carseto.com/v1/search?q=Porsche%20911', {
    headers: { 'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_KEY' }
  });
  const cars = await response.json();
  console.log(cars);
  ```
  ```bash
  curl "https://api.carseto.com/v1/search?q=Porsche%20911" \
    -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY"
  ```
  ```python
  import requests
  response = requests.get(
      'https://api.carseto.com/v1/search?q=Porsche%20911',
      headers={'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_KEY'}
  )
  cars = response.json()
  print(cars)
  ```
</CodeGroup>

## Add Your First Car to the Garage

Build your digital garage by adding personal classics—not for sale, but to track value and document history.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add Your Car" icon="plus">
    From your dashboard, select **Add Your Car**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="edit-3">
    Input make (`Jaguar`), model (`E-Type`), year (`1965`), VIN, and upload photos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Documentation" icon="file-text">
    Upload service records, invoices, and ownership history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Track" icon="save">
    Save the car. View monthly value updates based on real market data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Start with one car to see valuations in action. Values update monthly from 5+ marketplaces.
</Callout>

## Configure Valuations and Notifications

<Expandable title="Enable Notifications" default-open="true">
  Set up email alerts for value changes or matching listings:
  1. Go to **Settings > Notifications**.
  2. Toggle **Value Alerts** and **Similar Listings**.
  3. Enter your preferences (e.g., `>10% increase`).
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Valuation Settings">
  Customize portfolio insights:
  - Select markets (e.g., UK, Germany).
  - Choose condition grades ( concours, good).
  - Enable portfolio totals.
</Expandable>
