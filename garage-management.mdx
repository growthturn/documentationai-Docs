## Adding a Car to Your Garage

Follow these steps to add a classic car and begin tracking it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search and Select" icon="search">
    Use the search bar to find your car model, or enter details manually like "Porsche 911 1973".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="edit-3">
    Fill in VIN, mileage, condition, and acquisition date. Carseto auto-matches similar listings for valuation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save to Garage" icon="save">
    Click "Add to Garage". Your car now appears in your portfolio with an initial value estimate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Uploading Documents and Photos

Upload files directly from your device. Supported formats include JPEG, PNG for photos, and PDF for records.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Photos" icon="image">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select Photos">
        Navigate to your car's detail page and click "Upload Photos".
      </Step>
      <Step title="Organize">
        Drag and drop or select files. Add captions like "Engine restoration 2023".
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Service Records" icon="file">
    Upload PDFs of invoices and logs. Tag them by date or service type for easy search.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Ownership History" icon="users">
    Scan deeds or log transfers. Carseto timestamps uploads for provenance tracking.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Viewing Estimated Values and Insights

Access your dashboard to see real-time updates. Values pull from 5 European marketplaces, refreshed daily.

| Metric | Description | Update Frequency |
|--------|-------------|------------------|
| Current Value | Market-based estimate | Monthly |
| Collection Total | Sum of all cars | Real-time |
| Value Change | 12-month trend | Quarterly |
| Rarity Score | Based on listings | Daily |

<Expandable title="Advanced Insights" default-open="false">
  Use filters for model-specific trends or export CSV reports for tax purposes.
</Expandable>

## Updating Details and Tracking Changes

Keep your garage current as you restore or drive your cars.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Edit Details" icon="settings">
    Go to car details and update mileage or condition. Triggers value recalculation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log Events" icon="calendar">
    Add service notes or photos. View change history timeline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Market Alerts" icon="bell">
    Set notifications for value spikes or new similar listings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
